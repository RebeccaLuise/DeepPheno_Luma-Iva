# DeepPheno_Luma-Iva
This repo supports the final data analysis of the OrkambiKIDS study.     

OrkambiKIDS is a clinical phase IV study designed to monitor treatment with Lumacaftor/Ivacaftor of children aged 6 to 12 with cystic fibrosis (CF) homozygous for the F508del mutation. We aimed to assess changes in the intestinal and respiratory microbiome and metabolome in correlation with CF-typical biomarkers from stool, sputum and blood plasma during 24 months of study period at 9 different timepoints.

Clinical study register entry: https://drks.de/search/en/trial/DRKS00022267

Raw sequencing data (fastq files) is available from Sequence Read Archive (SRA), PRJNA1119982. The mass spectrometric data is available on https://massive.ucsd.edu under the accession number MassIVE MSV000094922.
